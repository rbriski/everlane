The Dockside Market Campaign

A Story of Sustainability and Style with Everlane

Our Campaign Strategy

We're partnering with Everlane to tell a unique story of transformation, sustainability, and preppycore fashion. This journey will be shared through social media and our website, focusing on how the brand's dedication to sustainable practices aligns with the creation of "The Dockside Market."

Our strategy includes:

  • Social Media Storytelling: We'll start by sharing Amelia's journey on Instagram and TikTok, using short videos and product shots to introduce Everlane's preppycore line. Each post will highlight different aspects of the story, from the initial inspiration to the market's grand reveal.
  • Website Integration: The posts will lead followers to this website, where they can dive deeper into the story through exclusive behind-the-scenes content, product showcases, and video narratives.
  • Hashtag Engagement: The campaign hashtag #DocksideRevival will encourage users to share their own stories of sustainable fashion, creating a community around Everlane's values.

Our Story

Amelia, inspired by the charm of her coastal hometown, envisioned transforming an old, run-down warehouse into a vibrant market. This journey was not just about creating a space but also about embracing sustainability and style.

With the help of local craftsmen, she began renovating the warehouse, infusing it with nautical-themed stalls made from reclaimed wood. As the market took shape, it became a perfect blend of preppycore aesthetics and eco-friendly design.

Today, "The Dockside Market" stands as a testament to the community's commitment to sustainability, featuring Everlane's preppycore fashion line that aligns perfectly with the market's ethos.
